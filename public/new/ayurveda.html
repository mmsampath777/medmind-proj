    Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)

    Uses - Stress relief, improving energy, enhancing overall vitality, boosting immunity, and improving cognitive function.

    Chemical Composition - Alkaloids (e.g., withanine), steroidal lactones (withanolides), saponins, iron, and amino acids.

    Places Found - Native to India, North Africa, and the Middle East; also cultivated in some parts of the USA.

    Medicinal Uses - Reduces cortisol levels, helping with stress and anxiety management.Improves physical endurance and muscle strength.

    Tulsi (Ocimum sanctum)

    Uses - Immunity booster, stress relief, improving respiratory health, and promoting skin health.

    Chemical Composition - Essential oils (e.g., eugenol, camphor), flavonoids, rosmarinic acid, and various polyphenols.

    Places Found - Widely cultivated in India, Southeast Asia, and tropical regions worldwide.

    Medicinal Uses - Promotes respiratory health by easing congestion and cough.Helps reduce fever and acts as a natural immune booster.

    Ginger

    Ginger is a flowering plant whose rhizome, commonly known as ginger root, is widely used as a spice and traditional medicine.

    The plant grows to about 3 to 4 feet tall, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and yellow-green flowers.

    The rhizome is harvested for its pungent, aromatic properties.

    Neem, (Indian Lilac)

    Neem is a fast-growing tree native to the Indian subcontinent, known for its broad, green leaves and small, white flowers.

    The seeds, leaves, and bark of the neem tree are widely used in traditional medicine for their antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties.

    Neem oil is especially valued for its medicinal and insecticidal uses.

    Aloe Vera(Ghritkumari)

    Aloe Vera is a succulent plant species known for its thick, fleshy leaves that contain a soothing gel.

    The gel is widely used for its healing, moisturizing, and anti-inflammatory properties.

    Aloe Vera is commonly grown in tropical climates and is a popular ingredient in skincare, health, and wellness products.
