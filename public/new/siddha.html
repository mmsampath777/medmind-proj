    Seenthil

    Seenthil is a woody, climbing plant native to tropical areas of India.

    It is highly regarded for its medicinal properties, especially in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

    The plant is known for its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellow flowers.

    Nathaisoori

    Nathaisoori is a herbaceous plant commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.

    It has slender, erect stems, small white or pink flowers, and typically grows in grasslands, roadsides, and waste areas.

    Pirandai

    Pirandai is a perennial plant that grows as a fleshy, quadrangular vine.

    Known for its healing properties, it has long been used in traditional medicine for its bone-strengthening and anti-inflammatory properties.

    Madder

    Madder is a perennial climbing herb widely known for its red roots, which have been used for dyeing fabrics for centuries.

    The plant can grow up to 1.5 meters long, with small yellow-green flowers and dark berries.

    Thanneer-vittan Kilangu

    Thanneer-vittan Kilangu, or Shatavari, is a perennial herb known for its tuberous roots.

    It is widely used in Ayurvedic medicine for its adaptogenic and reproductive health benefits.

    The plant is characterized by needle-like leaves, small white flowers, and globular berries.
