    Bhakti Yoga

    Bhakti Yoga is a spiritual path focused on love, devotion, and surrender to the Divine.

    It emphasizes cultivating a personal relationship with God through prayers, chanting, and rituals.

    Power Yoga

    Power Yoga is a fast-paced, intense yoga practice that combines traditional yoga poses with dynamic movements to improve strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular endurance.

    Yin Yoga

    Yin Yoga is a slow-paced yoga practice that focuses on long-held, passive stretches that target deep connective tissues, joints, and fascia.

    Unlike dynamic yoga styles, Yin Yoga emphasizes stillness and relaxation, helping to increase flexibility, improve circulation, and promote deep healing.

    Jnana Yoga

    Jnana Yoga is a path of deep introspection, and while it is generally safe, it is important to practice with guidance if you are new to the concepts of self-inquiry and philosophical study.

    Seek wisdom from experienced teachers and avoid getting lost in intellectual knowledge without applying it in practice.

    Karma yoga

    Karma Yoga is the path of selfless action, focusing on performing one's duties and actions without attachment to the results.

    It teaches that actions performed in the service of others or the divine can lead to spiritual growth and liberation.
